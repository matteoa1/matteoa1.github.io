Lucy When She's Hungry

Whenever my dog is hungry, she would use her persuasive puppy dog eyes abilities to beg for food. She'll constantly follow people around, and patiently sit to remind everyone it's time to eat. Finally, when she recieved her food, she's the happiest dog in the world. Once she's finished with her plate, you would always find her at the dinner table begging for more.
